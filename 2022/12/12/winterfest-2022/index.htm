Winterfest is back, and Santa needs your help!

Trouble in the Grotto

Santa is in trouble. A shortage of toy-making supplies has led to the elves falling behind with making this year's presents, and it doesn't look like the Winterfest preparations will be completed on time!

Santa and his elves need help assembling, checking, wrapping, and sorting this year's gifts.

Do you think you have what it takes to help Santa and save Winterfest? Speak to Mrs. Claus in the hub to get started!

Festive Surprises

Winterfest is all about giving! Starting on the 14th of December, be sure to join The Hive and check under the big Winterfest tree each day to receive a special daily gift.

There are twelve total gifts to collect. Each gift will only be available for 24 hours, and once gone, will never be available again - talk about exclusive!

Seasonal Maps

All of your favorite wintery maps are back once more, including some brand new festive variants.

There are over 50 festive worlds to explore, so wrap up warm and play some games!
