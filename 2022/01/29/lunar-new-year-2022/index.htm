It's time to celebrate Lunar New Year!

It's time to celebrate Lunar New Year, and the Year of the Tiger!

To ring in the new year, the hub has had a festive makeover! From the castle to the beaches, theres so much to explore!

Be sure to be on the lookout for special Zodiac banners that have been hidden throughout the hub. There are 12 to find, and each one depicts an animal of the Chinese Zodiac. There's an exclusive costume for those that manage to find them all!

A Lunar New Year celebration wouldn't be complete without the Lantern Festival... Make sure to join The Hive on the 15th of February to see beautiful lanterns lighting up the sky!

What's next?

The team has been hard at work preparing lots of awesome content for our next permanent updates! We can't wait to show you what we've been working on, be sure to check out our blog post to find out what's coming next.

From all of us at The Hive, have a fantastic Lunar New Year! 🏮
