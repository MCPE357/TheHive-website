What's an MMO? A massively multiplayer online game is an online video game with a large number of players on the same server. MMOs, including ours, usually feature a huge, persistent open world.

We've made the decision to launch this server fully separated from our Hive Minigames server. Whilst it's being created by the team behind The Hive, that's pretty much where the overlap ends.

The MMO being fully independent gives us a lot of freedom: it'll launch on an improved tech stack, whilst based on our Minigame server, with many improvements and new features.
